# Headline Formulas Reference

Conversion data compiled from Sumo (150,000+ headline study), Lovable.dev A/B test meta-analysis, LKR Social Media tests, and CXL research.

---

## Formula 1: Specific Result (Best for cold traffic)

**Structure:** `We Help [Niche] [Achieve Specific Outcome] in [Timeframe]`

**Why it works:** Hits dream outcome + specificity + timeframe + niche callout simultaneously. Number-based headlines are preferred by 36% of readers; dollar figures resonate strongly with business owners.

**Examples:**
- "We Help Supplement Brands Generate $100K+ in Monthly Email Revenue in 90 Days"
- "We Help eCom Brands Add $50K–$200K/Month in Email Revenue"

**Fill-in-the-blank:**
`We Help [Target Niche] Generate/Add/Achieve [Specific Dollar/Result] in [Timeframe]`

**Conversion note:** Specific, unrounded numbers ("$214,382" not "$200,000") increase credibility — they imply measurement rather than estimation.

---

## Formula 2: How To… Without… (Cold and warm traffic)

**Structure:** `How to [Desired Result] Without [Primary Objection]`

**Why it works:** Combines benefit with objection removal in a single headline. The "Without" pre-handles the #1 sales objection before the prospect even reads the body.

**Examples:**
- "How to 3X Your Email Revenue Without Hiring a Single In-House Marketer"
- "How to Scale eCom Email Revenue Without Agency Lock-In"

**Fill-in-the-blank:**
`How to [Desired Outcome] Without [Main Objection/Fear]`

---

## Formula 3: Case Study (Cold traffic — social proof priority)

**Structure:** `How [Specific Entity] [Achieved Specific Result] in [Timeframe]`

**Why it works:** Social proof is embedded directly in the headline. Prospects see themselves in the story before reading a single word of body copy. Ideal when the audience is skeptical.

**Examples:**
- "How One Supplement Brand Added $214K in Email Revenue in 60 Days"
- "How [Brand Name] Added $127K in Email Revenue in 60 Days"

**Fill-in-the-blank:**
`How [One/A] [Niche Entity] Added/Generated [Specific Result] in [Timeframe]`

---

## Formula 4: Testimonial Headline (Any traffic temperature)

**Structure:** `"[Client Quote]" — [Attribution]`

**Why it works:** Leading with a verbatim client quote as the headline delivered a **24.31% conversion lift** in LKR Social Media's controlled test. Feels authentic, not promotional.

**Examples:**
- "'They Added $83K in Email Revenue in Our First Month Together' — Brand Founder"
- "'We Went From $12K to $94K/Month in Email in 90 Days' — eCom Founder, [City]"

**Best practices:**
- Use the client's actual words, not a polished paraphrase
- Include attribution (role/title is more powerful than a name alone for privacy-sensitive prospects)
- Prioritize quotes that mention a specific dollar result or timeframe

---

## Formula 5: Authority Formula (Best for warm traffic)

**Structure:** `The [Role/Team] Behind [Proof of Results]`

**Why it works:** Works with warm traffic who already know the category and need expert positioning. Fails cold — prospects must already care about the category for authority to convert.

**Examples:**
- "The Email Marketing Team Behind 7 and 8-Figure Supplement Brands"
- "The Agency That Manages Email for Australia's Fastest-Growing eCom Brands"

**Fill-in-the-blank:**
`The [Team/Agency/Expert] Behind [Prestigious Proof Point]`

## A/B Test Suggestions

Always launch with at least two variants. Recommended starting split:

1. `We Help Supplement Brands Add $50K–$200K/Month in Email Revenue` *(Formula 1)*
2. `How One Supplement Brand Added $214K in Email Revenue in 60 Days` *(Formula 3)*

If volume allows, add:
3. `Stop Leaving 30–40% of Your Revenue on the Table` *(loss aversion / pattern interrupt)*

---

## Subheadline Rules

- **Length:** 15–25 words
- **Job:** Expand the headline promise and bridge into the first paragraph
- **Structure tip:** Subheadline should answer the natural "how?" or "really?" response the headline generates
- **Example pairing:**
  - Headline: "We Help Supplement Brands Generate $100K+ in Monthly Email Revenue in 90 Days"
  - Sub: "Our done-for-you email system combines advanced segmentation with conversion-focused sequences — without you touching a single workflow."
